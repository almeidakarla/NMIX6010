    About

    Studio Vert was born from the concept of bringing the serene and abundant energy of nature into your home and workplace. Our personalized frames showcase high-quality photographic artworks and Fine Art printing.

    In line with Environmental Psychology, pictures of nature have the potential to instill feelings of peace, concentration, and overall well-being in humans. This is attributed to research findings that suggest humans, due to their extensive history of dwelling in natural surroundings, experience improved well-being when immersed in such environments.

    Biophilia

    Biophilia is the innate human affinity and connection to nature.

    Aesthetic

    Incorporating biophilic elements into home decor enhances aesthetic appeal while fostering a sense of harmony and well-being with nature.

    Nature

    Embracing nature-inspired decor not only elevates the aesthetics of your home but also creates a calming sanctuary.
